# 🏥 Electronic Health Record (EHR) & Laboratory Management Portal

## 📌 Project Overview

The Electronic Health Record (EHR) & Laboratory Management Portal is a web-based healthcare management system designed to digitize patient records, laboratory operations, doctor consultations, prescriptions, appointments, and healthcare analytics.

The system eliminates paper-based record management and provides secure, centralized access to patient medical information, laboratory reports, and healthcare services.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Electronic Health Records (EHR)
* Doctor Consultation Management
* Prescription Management
* Laboratory Test Management
