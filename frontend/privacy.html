Privacy Policy & HIPAA Compliance

Your medical records and personal information are guarded to enterprise SOC2 and HIPAA standards.

Effective Date: June 2026 HIPAA Certified

At CurePoint, we prioritize the protection and security of your protected health info (PHI). This document explains how we collect, protect, share, and utilize patient information, in compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), SOC2 privacy standards, and local clinical data regulations.

1. Health Information We Collect

To deliver diagnostic care and medical scheduling services, CurePoint processes several forms of data:

  • Demographics & Contacts: Full name, national identity indices (Aadhaar or similar), birth dates, gender, residential address, telephone contact, and emergency contact registries.
  • Clinical Records: Electronic Health Records (EHR) containing diagnosis logs, consultation timelines, vital signs, drug allergy logs, treatment charts, and e-prescriptions.
  • Laboratory Diagnostics: Test orders, lab technician notes, vital reports, PDF compilations, and diagnostic metrics.
  • Billing & Payments: Consultation fees, payment logs, UPI transaction details, and invoicing files.

2. HIPAA Privacy Rules & Safe Harbor

In accordance with the HIPAA Privacy Rule, CurePoint enforces administrative, physical, and technical safeguards:

  • Access Controls: Role-based access ensures that only authenticated and authorized clinicians (doctors, lab technicians) or administrators can view a patient's electronic health records.
  • Audit Trails: Every system interaction (login, file view, diagnostic update, prescription release) triggers an immutable entry inside our audit logs to maintain absolute tracking.
  • Data Minimization: Any demographic summaries requested by analytics dashboards use aggregate tables, stripping away identifiable indicators where feasible.

3. Data Encryption & Security

All communication inside the CurePoint platform utilizes secure channels:

  • Encryption in Transit: Secure Sockets Layer/Transport Layer Security (SSL/TLS) tunnels encrypt all inputs submitted from dashboard widgets before they hit our Django backend APIs.
  • Encryption at Rest: All PostgreSQL database tables containing clinical charts are encrypted using advanced AES-256 standards.
  • Regular Audits: We undergo third-party penetration testing and vulnerability assessments to secure endpoints from registry risks.

4. Third-Party Sharing Policies

We do not sell, rent, or trade your medical data under any circumstances. Information is only shared under clear medical scenarios:

  • With your designated family physicians or referring clinical staff.
  • With authorized laboratory technicians compiling diagnostic findings.
  • With insurance companies requesting billing claims (subject to pre-authorization).
  • When required by statutory public health mandates.

5. Patient Rights & Access

As a patient using the CurePoint portal, you hold several rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Inspect: You can view all consultation history, vitals, prescriptions, and lab reports directly via your dashboard.
  • Right to Amend: You can update demographics, emergency contacts, blood group, and allergies, which will undergo backend validations.
  • Right to Export: You can download copies of your diagnostic reports and billing receipts in secure PDF formats.

6. Updates to This Policy

We reserves the right to modify this privacy framework to align with updated clinical compliance codes. Authenticated users will receive immediate dashboard alerts when updates are published.
